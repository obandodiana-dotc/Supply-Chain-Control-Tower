These indicators are the foundation for all other pages of the app:

  • Inventory page deep-dives into policies and multi-SKU behavior.
  • Logistics Map shows node-level performance and lead time risk.
  • Simulation explores day-by-day inventory dynamics.
  • Risk consolidates operational risk into an intuitive view.
  • ABC classifies products by economic impact and demand share.
App Modules
  • Multi-SKU Manager: EOQ, ROP, Safety Stock and risk per SKU.
  • Interactive Logistics Map: Supplier → Factory → DC → Store → Customer.
  • Demand Simulation: day-by-day inventory levels with stockout detection.
  • Canvas Chart: pure JavaScript chart for inventory profile.
  • Risk Dashboard: operational and financial risk in one view.
  • Dynamic ABC: clustering SKUs into A/B/C based on annual consumption.
How to use this app
  • Start with Inventory to understand SKU-level dynamics.
  • Use the Logistics Map to identify which nodes amplify risk.
  • Run a Simulation to see daily behavior under different parameters.
  • Review the Risk Dashboard to prioritize mitigation actions.
  • Apply ABC Clustering to focus effort on the most impactful products.
